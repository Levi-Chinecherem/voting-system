Welcome to School Voting System

School Voting System is an automated platform where students and staff can participate in voting for their preferred candidates. The system ensures a fair and transparent voting process.

Voting Period:

The voting period for each position is specified in the system. Users can only vote during the active voting period for each position. Once the voting period for a position ends, voting for that position will be closed.

To start voting, please log in using your registered account or register for a new account if you haven't already.

Login Register

System Details

The School Voting System is built using the Django web framework and the Bootstrap front-end framework. It provides an easy-to-use interface for students and staff to cast their votes for candidates vying for various positions in the school.

The system has the following features:

  • User authentication and registration using Django-Allauth, with options to login via Gmail and Facebook.
  • Displaying a list of candidates with their images, names, positions, and details.
  • Allowing users to vote for their preferred candidate.
  • Showing voting results with the number of votes and percentage for each candidate.
  • Preventing users from voting more than once and after the voting period has closed.

How to Use

Follow the steps below to use the School Voting System:

  1. Register: If you don't have an account, click on the "Register" link in the navigation bar and create a new account.
  2. Login: Once registered, use your email and password to log in to your account.
  3. View Candidates: After logging in, navigate to the "Candidates" page to view the list of candidates running for various positions.
  4. Vote: Click on the "Vote" link in the navigation bar to cast your vote for your preferred candidate.
  5. View Results: After the voting period has ended, you can check the "Results" page to see the voting results and the winner for each position.
